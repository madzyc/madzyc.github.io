Madison Cox

Freshman Broadcast and Digital Journalism Student at Syracuse University

Madison was born in St. Louis Missouri, but has always loved the East Coast, which is what brought her to Syracuse. A freshman in BDJ, she is also the Web and Digital Media Director for CitrusTV, a Staff Photographer for the Daily Orange, a photographer for JERK Magazine, and a member of the Social Media Team for Live From Studio B. Madison plans to minor in Photojournalism and frequently takes on freelance photo gigs that she then posts to her journalism instagram @maddylearnsjournalism.
